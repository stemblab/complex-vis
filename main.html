Visualization of Complex Functions

Sometimes called [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domain_coloring, domain coloring], complex functions can be visualized by mapping the argument and phase to a color model. In the mapping below we use the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HSL_and_HSV, HSLA] model, because it is consistent with HTML canvas. The hue (H) is mapped to the argument. The saturation (S), lightness (L) and opacity (A) are editable and are roughly consistent with [http://www.cs.amherst.edu/~djv/FTAp.pdf, Velleman's approach].
